About Us

Language learning is not and will never be a game to those who are serious. Language Soup was created by Noah Aire, a life-long language learner. Having studied French and Business at Georgetown University, he realized that adults struggle to structure their language learning outside of the classroom. Taking a year to pursue a Fulbright Research Grant, Noah was able to dive deeper into the lived experience of business, language and culture. Through series of interviews, tapping into his 10 years of language learning and understanding the research, "The Language Learning Journal" was born. Noah realized that serious language learners did not need more apps, they needed a way to structure, document and track their learning the only way that they trust: pen & paper. Through multiple iterations, the first version of the product geared towards French langauge learners has been released. Finally a language learning solution for language learners by language learners.
